About Me

Hello World! I'm Troy Tsubota, an iOS developer who resides in Southern California.

I am currently an eighth grader at Corona del Mar Middle and High School. I participate in the school's student body and am always involved in the school. Outside of school, I'm typically busy programming or learning new things.

I started programming with basic HTML, CSS, and Javascript 5 years ago before moving over to iOS two years later. Although familiar with Objective-C, my preferred programming language is Swift.

Outside of Xcode, I am on a club swim team that practices for 3 hours six days a week. I also enjoy hacking around in open source Swift on my Raspberry Pi 2.

WWDC Scholar 2016–2017 (check out my Medium article about it).
