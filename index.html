Flowtrend

February 2018

Your personal asthma assistant, on your iPhone, for free. Flowtrend is a native Swift asthma management app. The various visuals combined with the vibrant and consistent color scheme provide valuable data visualization for the user.

  • Features
  • Medication reminders
  • Peak flow log
  • Rescue inhaler tracking
  • Record your symptoms
  • Asthma action plan
  • Time-based contextual actions
  • Treatment/measurement history
  • Technologies
  • CareKit
  • HealthKit
  • Local notifications
  • Auto Layout
  • Dynamic Type
  • Core Graphics

ScholarPlan

December 2014

As my first iOS app, ScholarPlan is a student planner for students to get organized. Its simple user interface is designed for quick interaction. ScholarPlan is built completely from scratch with 100% of the codebase in Swift.

  • Track your assignments
  • Due dates with notifications
  • Categorize by type
  • Organize your classes
  • Technologies

  • NSCoding/NSKeyedArchiver
